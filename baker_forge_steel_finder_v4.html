Why add extra steel?

When you grind bevels and clean up your profile, you lose material on the edges. If you order a billet that's exactly your finished blade size, you'll grind right through your final shape.

Leaving this checked adds 20% extra to length and width so you have working room to profile, grind bevels, and clean up. For example, a 4" × 1.25" finished blade becomes a 4.75" × 1.5" billet search.
